						Carbon Emission Analysis Project


Which industry had the highest CO2 emissions in the most recent year?
	SELECT industry, emission_amount
FROM emissions
WHERE gas_type = 'CO2'
AND year = (SELECT MAX(year) FROM emissions)
ORDER BY emission_amount DESC
LIMIT 1;

2. What has been the trend of total emissions across sectors over the last decade? 
	SELECT year,
       SUM(emission_amount) AS total_emissions
FROM emissions
GROUP BY year
ORDER BY year;

3. Which industries showed a decline in emissions over the year?
	SELECT industry,
       MIN(emission_amount) AS lowest_emission,
       MAX(emission_amount) AS highest_emission
FROM emissions
GROUP BY industry
HAVING MAX(emission_amount) > MIN(emission_amount);

4. How does the emission share differ across continents or economic zones?
	SELECT continent,
       SUM(emission_amount) AS total_emission
FROM emissions
GROUP BY continent
ORDER BY total_emission DESC;

5. Identify the top three emitting industries and their year-wise emission pattern.
	SELECT industry,
       year,
       SUM(emission_amount) AS yearly_emission
FROM emissions
GROUP BY industry, year
ORDER BY yearly_emission DESC;
